Travel Tips for India

A few simple tips can make your trip smoother and safer, especially if you are traveling long distances or across seasons.

  • Pack layers to handle different climates in one trip.
  • Keep digital and printed copies of ID cards and permits.
  • Try local food, but drink only clean or bottled water.
  • Use official or trusted transport and booking channels.
