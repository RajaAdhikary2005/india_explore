Travel Tips for India

Short practical tips that apply across the destinations listed here.

  • Carry photocopies of ID and offline maps.
  • Try local food from busy stalls but drink packaged water.
  • Book permits ahead for restricted areas (Ladakh, parts of NE India).
  • Respect local customs at religious sites.
