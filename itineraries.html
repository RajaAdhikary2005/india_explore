How to choose the right itinerary

1 day

Good for quick city overviews or when you are passing through and want only the main highlights.

2 days

Best balance for most destinations: one busy day, one slower day. Great for Delhi, Varanasi, Shimla and Rishikesh.

3+ days

Use for hill stations, Meghalaya, Rajasthan and Ladakh trips where travel time and altitude need more buffer.
